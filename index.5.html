About

Turning user problems into product outcomes.

Two live products shipped solo. Five deep PM case studies. Three years of professional experience across three countries worth of perspective — all before finishing my master's. I'm Gokul. I'm an MSc International Business student at EMLV, a triple-accredited Grande École in La Défense, and I've spent every week since arriving in Paris building the kind of portfolio most PMs promise but never deliver. Cleo is an AI guide I built for 400,000+ international students navigating French admin. PlayPal is a sports social app connecting local players. Both are live. Both are mine. Before Paris, I worked in Bengaluru across product support and customer success — where I learned that the best product insights come from the people filing complaints, not the people drawing roadmaps. That's still how I think. Start with the frustrated user. Work backwards. I'm looking for internship or full-time roles in Product Management, Marketing, or Customer Success in Paris. If you've read this far, you already know I don't wait around. Let's meet — I'll bring the case studies, the prototypes, and probably too much enthusiasm.
