The Problem

"Your first month in Paris should be exciting. Not spent in waiting rooms."

Every year, 400,000+ international students land in France and immediately face a wall of bureaucracy — CAF, CPAM, OFII, bank accounts, prefecture appointments — all in a language they barely speak, with no roadmap. Most spend 3–4 weeks just figuring out where to start. I lived this problem firsthand. Then I built the solution.

What Cleo Does — MVP v1
🤖
AI Chat Assistant
Ask anything about settling in Paris — answered in plain English. Dual AI (Gemini + Groq) with automatic failover for 99.9% uptime.
📋
24 Services Portal
Prefecture, CAF, CPAM, OFII, Navigo, banks and more — every service with direct booking links, tips, and location info.
📄
Document Scanner
Upload any French administrative letter. Get an instant English translation and a list of action items to complete.
✅
Personalised 7-Day Plan
5-step onboarding (nationality, university, visa, budget, housing) generates a day-by-day settlement checklist tailored to you.
