About

Built to ship. Wired to think.

Two live products. Five deep PM case studies. Three years of professional experience across India and France — all before finishing my master's. I'm Gokul — a product and growth operator working at the intersection of user insight, data, and execution. I built Cleo, an AI assistant for the 400,000+ international students navigating French bureaucracy each year. I built PlayPal, a sports social app for local player discovery. Both are live. Both started with a frustrated user and ended with a shipped product. Before Paris, I spent two years in Bengaluru in product support and customer success — where I learned that the sharpest product insights live in support tickets, not strategy decks. That's still how I work: start with the friction, map the system, fix the root cause. I use AI as an operational advantage — Claude for strategy synthesis and PRDs, Lovable and Cursor for shipping fast, Perplexity for real-time market intelligence. In a world where ideas are cheap, execution speed is the differentiator. I'm targeting Product Management, Growth, and Product Marketing roles at ambitious startups in Paris. If you've read this far, you already know I don't wait around.
