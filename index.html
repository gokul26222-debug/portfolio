About

Turning user problems into product outcomes.

I'm a Product Manager based in Paris with 3+ years of experience spanning early-stage startups and established consumer platforms. My work sits at the intersection of user research, data analysis, and cross-functional execution — I translate ambiguous problems into structured product strategies that move metrics. I've tackled challenges across retention, onboarding, and growth for companies like Joko, FAUME, Netflix (case study), and BlaBlaCar (teardown).
